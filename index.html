Are you struggling to photograph backyard birds flying?

Hi I’m Scott Prince, and welcome to my site. I’ve been involved in photography for over 40 years photographing weddings, headshots, and pageants. Recently I turned to wildlife as I find it to be very challenging, yes even more challenging than a wedding.

I started out in my early teens creating photos for friends and relatives and knew then photography was my passion.

In 2003 I was the official headshot photographer for MISS MARYLAND USA, MISS MARYLAND USA TEEN, MISS NEW JERSEY USA, AND MISS NEW JERSEY TEEN USA. During that time, I also created head shots for local inspiring actors and models from the Baltimore area.

I also trained professionally as a wedding photographer for about a year and went out on my own for about 5 years.

A few years ago I was a volunteer photographer for a local animal shelter helping dogs and cats get adopted, that was very rewarding.

So now, I go out when I can and photograph birds and anything else I find interesting.

Hope you enjoy my images and thanks for stopping by!

"Scott"
